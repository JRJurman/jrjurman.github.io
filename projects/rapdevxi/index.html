RapDevXI

This RapDev Project was a weekend long project where SSE members built small minigames which were bundled and played in quick sucession (akin to Nintendo’s WarioWare Series). I helped build the initial prototypes and high-level design. We had over 15 members contribute to the project, and while development of the minigames was only over one weekend, the modular design made the project a success. We had 30 minigames, complete with music, sounds, and animations, submitted in total. The games were built using lua and the 2D Game Engine Love. [GitHub Page]
